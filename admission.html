◉ Admission FAQ

What are the eligibility criteria for B.Ed?

Candidates must have at least 50% marks in Bachelor's or Master's Degree as per State Government rules.

Can I apply while waiting for final year results?

Yes, subject to submission of final qualifying documents within the prescribed timeline.

Is there an application fee?

Application fees are shown in the application portal before payment.

What documents are required for verification?

Academic certificates, identity proof, photographs, and category documents where applicable.
